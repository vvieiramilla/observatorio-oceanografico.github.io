> **Important note**  
> The **Lattes Platform** is a Brazilian academic information system, maintained
> in Portuguese, and widely used in Brazil for academic evaluation, funding
> decisions, and career progression.  
> As such, this tool is primarily relevant to the Brazilian academic context and
> does not directly apply to countries where academic assessment relies on
> different evaluation systems and metrics.

      Brazilian Lattes Curriculum, integrating indicators from the
      <em>Journal Citation Reports (JCR)</em>.

This resource was designed to support scientific output analysis,
institutional evaluations, and bibliometric studies, reducing manual effort
and increasing the reproducibility of analytical workflows.

### Main features
- Automatic extraction of data from the Lattes Curriculum;
- Integration with Journal Citation Reports (JCR) metrics;
- Generation of structured tables and analysis-ready outputs;
- Open-source and reusable codebase.

### Access to the code
👉 The source code, technical documentation, and usage instructions are
available on GitHub:
