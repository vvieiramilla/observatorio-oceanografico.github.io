The Observatório Oceanográfico (O²/UFF) is pleased to share that **our coordinator, Prof. André Luiz Belem**, has been recognized by the journal **_Evolving Earth_ (Elsevier)** as one of the **editor-selected reviewers** for the **2024–2025** cycle.

The recognition was announced in the journal’s official statement, available at:  
🔗 https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/evolving-earth/about/announcements/celebrating-our-reviewer-community-and-editors-selections-for-2024-2025

According to Elsevier, the selection highlights reviewers who demonstrated **excellence, dedication, and scientific rigor** in the evaluation of manuscripts, contributing directly to editorial quality and to the advancement of international research in Earth Sciences.

Prof. André Belem appears alongside researchers from institutions such as **Yale University**, **Federal University of Rio de Janeiro (UFRJ)**, **St. Petersburg State University**, **Technische Universität Bergakademie Freiberg**, **LSCE**, among others — a recognition that reinforces the global engagement and academic commitment of O².

We remain proud of the active participation of our members in the scientific community and of our contribution to strengthening the culture of peer review, which is essential for robust, ethical, and collaborative science.
