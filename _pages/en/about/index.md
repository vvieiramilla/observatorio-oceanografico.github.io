<h2 class="hero-subtitle">About the Oceanographic Observatory</h2>

The Oceanographic Observatory (O₂) is an interdisciplinary laboratory affiliated with the Fluminense Federal University (UFF), operating in research, education, outreach, and innovation related to marine sciences, climate change, and environmental technologies.
