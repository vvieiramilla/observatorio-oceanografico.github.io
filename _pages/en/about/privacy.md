## Privacy Policy

The **Oceanographic Observatory of the Federal Fluminense University (UFF)** respects the privacy of visitors and is committed to transparency regarding data usage.

### Data collection and use

This website uses **Google Analytics 4** exclusively for **institutional and statistical purposes**, in order to:

- understand how pages are accessed and used;
- improve website content and structure;
- support outreach related to **research, teaching, and extension activities**.

All collected information is **aggregated and anonymized**, and does not allow personal identification.

### What we do **not** collect

- No sensitive personal data is collected;
- No advertising, remarketing, or individual profiling is performed;
- No cookies are used for commercial purposes.

### Legal basis

Data processing is based on **legitimate institutional interest**, in accordance with applicable data protection regulations.
